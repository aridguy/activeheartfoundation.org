Breaking barriers: transforming the world

Empowering Nigeria's future

At Active Heart Foundation, we believe every child, youth, widow, less privileged individual, and person with physical challenges deserves a chance to thrive. Our mission is to provide inclusive education, mentorship, and community support to unlock their potential and drive positive change.

    Our Focus

  • Education: Equipping children and youth with skills for a brighter future
  • Mentorship: Guiding them towards self-discovery and purpose
  • Community: Supporting widows, less privileged individuals, and persons with physical challenges with resources and care
  • Empowerment: Unlocking potential, fostering independence, and promoting social impact.

Our Story

Active Heart Foundation was founded by Mr. Osuigwe Onyebuchi Collins, a Nigerian diaspora living in the United States, who was inspired by his experiences growing up in Nigeria and his passion for education and youth development.

Mr. Osuigwe Onyebuchi Collins, collaborated with a team of dedicated professionals in Nigeria to establish Active Heart Foundation, with the goal of providing quality education and mentorship to disadvantaged Nigerian children, youth, men and women.

Our Core Values

  • Inclusivity: We believe everyone deserves equal opportunities and support.
  • Empowerment: We empower individuals to take control of their lives and futures.
  • Compassion: We care deeply about the well-being and success of our beneficiaries.
  • Integrity: We operate with transparency, honesty, and accountability.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in all our programs and services.

Our Programs

  • Education: After-school programs, scholarships, and literacy initiatives.
  • Mentorship: One-on-one mentoring, group mentoring, and peer mentoring.
  • Community Support: Food bank, healthcare services, and economic empowerment programs.
